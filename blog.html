Netflix's Cowboy Bebop Fails?

November 20, 2021
Click for summary

3, 2, 1… Let’s Jam. The much awaited live action remake by Netflix has seemed to have failed with audiences everywhere. Just like everyone else I too was a little disappointed with it but nonetheless, I did enjoy it and thought it was good, not great but good. The show stayed true to the original anime in which we can all agree was awesome and rare to see. The cast and crew did a great job bringing the show to life but fight scenes and acting at times seemed dull and fake to me. Certain scenes came directly from the anime series but just felt different. But for someone looking for a decent show to watch with some ok fight scenes/entertainment then I recommend this show to you. All I have to say is that certain shows shouldn’t be remade because nothing beats the original. See you space cowboy.
